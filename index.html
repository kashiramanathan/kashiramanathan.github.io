Building Trust-First AI Products in Safety-Critical Operations

FireGrid — From unstructured maintenance logs to actionable safety signals

The Problem

Maintenance logs were free-text, handwritten, full of shorthand and inconsistent terminology. Critical safety signals were buried in noise. Rules-based logic failed due to variable technician language.

North Star & Principles

  • ✓ Trust > Automation
  • ✓ Human-in-the-Loop by Design
  • ✓ Start Simple, Scale Responsibly

The AI Solution

NLP/LLM + RAG to extract Asset ID, component/failure type, severity, and suggested next actions — always requiring human approval.

Why RAG not fine-tuning? Grounded in real manuals, auditable, knowledge evolves without retraining.
Hallucination mitigation Restricted context + confidence-based UX + workflow guardrails. AI never auto-triggers critical actions.

What We Deliberately Said No To

Predictive maintenance — too high validation cost and safety risk at current data maturity.
