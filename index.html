Centralized AI Orchestration for Safety-Critical SaaS

The Problem: Shadow AI & Data Fragmentation

In a multi-market SaaS environment, "Shadow AI" led to bot sprawl and inconsistent outputs. Safety signals were buried in free-text technician logs, making triage reactive and slow.

The Solution: Trust-First AI Orchestrator

  • Semantic Understanding: Deployed LLM + RAG to classify technician language without black-box drift.
  • Governance-by-Design: Built an 8-step AI lifecycle with automated guardrails for Legal & InfoSec approval.
  • Human-in-the-Loop: Designed a "Confidence UX" where AI suggests and humans decide, ensuring accountability.
