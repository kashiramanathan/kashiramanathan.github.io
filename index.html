My recent obsession has been building Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems that ground LLM outputs in regulatory data, service manuals, and historical incidents — delivering 75% earlier detection of critical asset issues and 25% less operational downtime.

🔥 The Real Problem

Maintenance logs were free-text, handwritten, full of technician shorthand, and scattered across disconnected systems. Critical safety signals were lost in operational noise. Triage was reactive, slow, and entirely manual.

Failure mode
